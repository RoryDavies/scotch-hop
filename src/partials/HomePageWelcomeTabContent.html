About Us

For many years, Linlithgow Scotch Hop has operated successfully as an informal committee.

The committee has now decided to convert to a SCIO (Scottish Charitable Incorporated Organisation), regulated by OSCR (Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator).
This will make no difference to the way Scotch Hop runs each year, but formalises how the committee does things, ensures we act correctly, and protects us as individuals acting in good faith, if Scotch Hop should ever find itself in financial difficulties.
Most of the previous Committee will be Charity Trustees of the SCIO.

The details of our SCIO registration are :
Full name: Linlithgow Scotch Hop SCIO
Charity number SCO46165

You can find more details of what this means at the OSCR website
